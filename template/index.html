DEATH TIME CALCULATOR

This calculator only takes 4 inputs:
- Ts (room temperature),
- t (time),
- T0 (object's initial temperature),
- T(t) (object's temperature at t time)
The expected output is the duration of time between the victim's death and the time of the time when the initial temperature was collected. After filling all the inputs, click "Submit" and Newton will calculate the answer.
For more information -> https://docs.google.com/document/d/1otPMFccOzKCUw03RuIXJCEMny8UKj05fykq1vtAqK94/edit?usp=sharing

E.g.: A victim died in a room with a tempreature of 25 degrees Celcius. The detective measured the body's initial tempreature, which was 30 degrees Celcius. After 10 minutes went by, he measured it again and got 28 degress Celcius.
Input: Ts = 25, t = 10, T0 = 30, T(t) = 28
